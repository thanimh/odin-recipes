Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

This chocolate chip cookie recipe is truly the best. These chocolate chip cookies are beloved because they're soft, chewy, and absolutely irresistible. Our top-rated recipe for chocolate chip cookies will quickly become your go-to.

Ingredients

  • 2 sticks butter
  • 3 eggs
  • Vanilla
  • 2 tablespoons baking soda
  • pinch ofsalt
  • 4 cupsflour
  • 1 cupsflour

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.

  2. Pre heat oven to 150 degrees C

  3. Beat the butter and sugars, then beat in the eggs and vanilla.

  4. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to the mixture.

  5. Stir in the flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts.

  6. Drop dough onto a prepared baking sheet.

  7. Bake until the edges are golden brown.

  8. Wait 5 minutes for the cookies to cool down then enjoy!.
